Our Journey

From Consultancy
to Platform.

Tecnix began with a simple observation: Ugandan businesses were struggling to access technology that actually worked for them. Imported software required dollar payments, assumed reliable internet, and offered support from teams thousands of miles away in completely different time zones.

We started by helping businesses navigate URA compliance, company registration, and ICT infrastructure. As we worked alongside our clients, the same problems kept surfacing — payroll being run on spreadsheets, stock managed on paper, invoices typed in Word documents. The tools existed globally, but none of them were built for here.

"We didn't set out to build software. We set out to solve problems. The software turned out to be the best solution."

That realisation led to the development of the TecnixOS Platform — four integrated products (Tecnix Ledger, Tecnix Roster, Tecnix Stock, and Tecnix POS) built from scratch for Uganda's real operating conditions: intermittent internet, mobile money payments, URA filing requirements, and UGX as the base currency.

Today, Tecnix operates as a full-service technology company that combines a growing SaaS platform with the professional services that Ugandan businesses continue to need. We believe software and human expertise are not alternatives — they are partners.
